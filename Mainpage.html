Amritsar

Amritsar is a city in the northern state of Punjab, India. It's known for its Golden Temple, a sacred Sikh shrine.

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is an iconic white marble mausoleum located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the Seven Wonders of the World.

Kerala

Kerala, often referred to as "God's Own Country," is a state in South India known for its lush greenery, backwaters, and diverse wildlife.

Himalayas

The Himalayas are a vast mountain range in Asia, covering several countries including India. They are home to some of the world's highest peaks.

Cochin

Cochin, also known as Kochi, is a major port city in the southwestern state of Kerala. It's famous for its rich history, vibrant culture, and beautiful coastal areas.

Ooty

Ooty, short for Ootacamund, is a hill station in the southern state of Tamil Nadu. It's known for its scenic beauty, tea gardens, and pleasant climate.

Rajasthan

Rajasthan is a state in northern India famous for its royal palaces, historic forts, and desert landscapes.

Thanjavur

Thanjavur, also known as Tanjore, is a city in Tamil Nadu known for its rich cultural heritage, including classical music and art.
